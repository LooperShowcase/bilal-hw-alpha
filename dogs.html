Dogs

A husky is a sled dog used in the polar regions. One can differentiate huskies from other dog types by their fast pulling-sty le.[citation needed] When used as a sled dog, they represent an ever-changing crossbreed of the fastest dogs[1] (the Alaskan Malamute, by contrast, pulled heavier loads at a slower speed).[2] Humans use huskies in sled-dog racing. Various companies have marketed tourist treks with dog sledges for adventure travelers in snow regions.[3] Huskies are also kept as pets, and groups work to find new pet homes for retired racing and adventure-trekking dogs.[4]
